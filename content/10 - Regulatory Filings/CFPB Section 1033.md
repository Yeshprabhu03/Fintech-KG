# CFPB Section 1033

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The most significant move toward open banking in the United States — a rule that mandates how banks must share customer data with third parties.

## Key Concepts
- **Dodd-Frank Section 1033**: The legislative mandate for consumer data access.
- **Open Banking Rule**: What the CFPB requires banks to do regarding data portability.
- **Timeline**: The multi-year phased rollout for banks of different sizes.
- **Fintech Impact**: How this rule legitimizes data aggregators and lowers switching costs between banks.
