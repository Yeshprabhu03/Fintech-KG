# MiCA (crypto regulation EU)

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) regulation is the EU's comprehensive framework for crypto-asset service providers (CASPs) and issuers of asset-referenced tokens and e-money tokens.

## Key Concepts
- **CASP Licensing**: The requirement for crypto exchanges, custodians, and advisors to obtain a license that is passportable across the EU.
- **Stablecoin Requirements**: Strict rules on the issuance of "Asset-Referenced Tokens" (ARTs) and "E-money Tokens" (EMTs), including mandatory reserves and redemption rights.
- **Consumer Protection**: Mandating that crypto-asset issuers provide clear and transparent "White Papers" describing the risks.
- **Environmental Disclosure**: Requiring CASPs to disclose the energy intensity and environmental impact of the crypto-assets they support.
