# EU AI Act impact on fintech

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of how the European Union's Artificial Intelligence Act regulates the use of AI in financial services, particularly regarding credit scoring and biometric identification.

## Key Concepts
- **High-Risk AI Systems**: Why credit scoring and identity verification AI are categorized as "high-risk" under the Act.
- **Transparency Obligations**: The requirement for fintechs to explain how their AI models reach a decision (explainability).
- **Prohibited AI**: The ban on specific AI uses, such as social scoring and real-time remote biometric ID in public spaces.
- **Compliance Deadlines**: The phased timeline for companies to reach compliance before facing significant fines (e.g., up to 7% of global turnover).
