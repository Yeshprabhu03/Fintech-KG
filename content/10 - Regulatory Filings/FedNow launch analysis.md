# FedNow launch analysis

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of the 2023 launch of FedNow, the Federal Reserve's real-time payment rail, and its competitive stance against The Clearing House (TCH) and legacy ACH.

## Key Concepts
- **Public vs. Private Rails**: The Federal Reserve's entrance into the RTP market, which was previously dominated by the private sector's TCH RTP.
- **Liquidity Management**: The challenge of ensuring banks have enough funds in their master accounts to settlement 24/7/365.
- **Interoperability Gap**: The lack of direct interoperability between FedNow and TCH RTP, requiring banks to connect to both or use a gateway.
- **Fraud Controls**: The initial (and evolving) fraud prevention tools provided to banks at launch.
