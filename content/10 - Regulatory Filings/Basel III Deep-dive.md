# Basel III Deep-dive

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
A deep-dive into the regulatory requirements of Basel III, focusing on the specific "Endgame" (Basel IV) rules and their impact on global banking stability.

## Key Concepts
- **Risk-Weighted Assets (RWA)**: the complex calculation used to determine how much capital a bank must hold against different types of loans.
- **Capital Buffers**: The requirement for banks to hold extra capital during good times (Countercyclical Capital Buffer) to use during downturns.
- **The "Basel III Endgame"**: The final round of post-2008 reforms aimed at further strengthening capital requirements for the largest institutions.
- **Fintech Arbitrage**: How stricter bank capital requirements create opportunities for shadow banks and fintech lenders to capture market share in riskier asset classes.
