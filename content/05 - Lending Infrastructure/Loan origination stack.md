# Loan origination stack

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The combination of software and data services that manage a loan from the initial application through underwriting and final disbursement.

## Key Concepts
- **LOS (Loan Origination System)**: The core workflow engine that manages the application lifecycle.
- **Decisioning Engines**: The rules-based or AI-driven systems that automatically approve or reject applicants.
- **Verification Rails**: Services that verify income (Plaid, Pinwheel), employment, and identity in real-time.
- **E-signature & Documentation**: Ensuring the legal enforceability of the digital loan contract.
