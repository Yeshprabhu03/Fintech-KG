# Securitization basics

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The financial process of pooling various types of contractual debt (such as loans or credit card debt) and selling their related cash flows to third-party investors as securities.

## Key Concepts
- **Asset-Backed Securities (ABS)**: The specific financial instruments created through the securitization of loan pools.
- **SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle)**: The off-balance-sheet legal entity that holds the assets and issues the securities.
- **Tranching**: Breaking the pool into different risk levels (Senior, Mezzanine, Equity) to appeal to different types of investors.
- **Fintech Liquidity**: Why securitization is the key "exit" for fintech lenders to get loans off their books and free up capital to lend more.
