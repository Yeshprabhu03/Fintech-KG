# Buy Now Pay Later mechanics

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The "Pay-in-4" or short-term installment loan model that allows consumers to purchase items and pay for them over a set period, often without interest.

## Key Concepts
- **The "Pay-in-4" Model**: Typically 25% down at checkout, followed by three bi-weekly installments.
- **Merchant Subsidy**: Why BNPL providers often charge merchants 3-6% (higher than standard credit card fees) in exchange for higher conversion.
- **Underwriting at the Edge**: How BNPL companies (Affirm, Klarna, Afterpay) make credit decisions in milliseconds using non-traditional data.
- **Regulatory Pressure**: Why the CFPB is moving toward treating BNPL like credit card providers regarding consumer protections.
