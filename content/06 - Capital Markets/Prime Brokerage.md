# Prime Brokerage

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The specialized suite of services traditional banks and broker-dealers offer to institutional investors like hedge funds.

## Key Concepts
- **Margin Lending**: Providing leverage so institutions can take larger positions.
- **Securities Lending**: Facilitating short selling by lending out restricted or unavailable stock.
- **Reporting & Cash Management**: Consolidating a fund's portfolio data and managing its operational cash.
- **Fintech Relevance**: Modern prime broker challengers are attempting to build for smaller funds and crypto institutions.
