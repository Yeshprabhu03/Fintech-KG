# HFT infrastructure

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The specialized hardware and low-latency networking required to execute high-frequency trading (HFT) strategies, where milliseconds (or microseconds) equate to profit.

## Key Concepts
- **Colocation**: Placing a firm's servers in the same physical data center as the exchange's matching engine.
- **FPGA & Microwave**: Using Field Programmable Gate Arrays (custom hardware) and microwave towers (faster than fiber) to minimize signal latency.
- **Matching Engines**: The centralized software at the exchange that matches buy and sell orders.
- **Tick-to-Trade Latency**: The measurement of time from receiving a market data "tick" to sending a reactive trade order.
