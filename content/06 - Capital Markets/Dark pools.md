# Dark pools

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
Private financial exchanges (Alternative Trading Systems or ATS) that allow institutional investors to trade large volumes of securities without revealing their intentions to the public market until after the trade is executed.

## Key Concepts
- **Price Improvement**: The promise of getting a better price than the public National Best Bid and Offer (NBBO) by trading within the pool.
- **Minimal Market Impact**: Allowing institutions to sell "blocks" of stock without triggering a massive price drop on public exchanges.
- **Lack of Transparency**: Why critics argue that dark pools create a fragmented market and disadvantage retail investors who only see the public tape.
- **HFT Interaction**: How high-frequency traders sometimes use dark pools to find institutional "whales" to trade against.
