# Algorithmic stablecoins (and failures)

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
Stablecoins that attempt to maintain their peg through automated, rules-based supply and demand mechanisms rather than 1:1 fiat reserves.

## Key Concepts
- **Seigniorage Shares**: The supply-adjustment mechanism where the protocol prints or burns a secondary token to stabilize the primary stablecoin.
- **Collateralization Ratios**: Comparing over-collateralized (DAI) vs. under-collateralized (UST/Terra) models.
- **Death Spiral Dynamics**: How a loss of confidence can trigger a feedback loop that destroys both the stablecoin and its reserve asset.
- **Failures (Terra/UST)**: The high-profile collapse of the Terra ecosystem in 2022 and its impact on the broader crypto and fintech market.
