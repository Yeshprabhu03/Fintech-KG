# Lightning Network

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The Layer 2 scaling solution for Bitcoin that enables near-instant, low-fee P2P transactions by moving them off-chain into payment channels.

## Key Concepts
- **Payment Channels**: Two-party channels where transactions occur off-chain and only the final balance is settled on the Bitcoin blockchain.
- **Routing & Nodes**: How payments are "routed" through a network of connected nodes to reach the intended recipient.
- **Liquidity Management**: The challenge of ensuring nodes have enough inbound and outbound capacity to facilitate flow.
- **Fintech Integration**: How companies like Strike are using Lightning to facilitate global remittances with Bitcoin as the rail.
