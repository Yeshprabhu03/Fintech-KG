# CBDC Overview

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The central bank-issued version of digital currency, representing a potential fundamental restructuring of the money supply.

## Key Concepts
- **Retail vs. Wholesale CBDC**: Digital cash for individuals vs. digital reserves for banks.
- **Global Pilots**: China (e-CNY), Bahamas (Sand Dollar), and the Euro/Dollar pilots.
- **Fintech Threat or Opportunity?**: Will CBDCs displace private stablecoins or provide a more stable foundation for apps?
- **Privacy & Control**: The debate over state monitoring of financial transactions via CBDC rails.
