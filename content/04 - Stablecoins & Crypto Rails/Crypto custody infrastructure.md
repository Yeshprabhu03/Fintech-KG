# Crypto custody infrastructure

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The specialized security and operational frameworks required to store digital assets safely and satisfy the "Qualified Custodian" requirements for institutional investors.

## Key Concepts
- **MPC (Multi-Party Computation)**: Splitting a private key into shards so no single entity can authorize a transaction.
- **Cold vs. Hot Storage**: Offline hardware-based security for long-term holdings vs. online systems for active trading.
- **Fiduciary Responsibility**: The regulatory requirement for chartered banks or trust companies to protect assets on behalf of clients.
- **Custody Players**: Comparing technology providers (Fireblocks, Copper) vs. full-service custodians (Coinbase Custody, BitGo, Anchorage).
