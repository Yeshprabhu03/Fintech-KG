# USDC vs USDT mechanics

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The two most dominant fiat-backed stablecoins (USDC by Circle and USDT by Tether), comparing their transparency, reserve composition, and regulatory posture.

## Key Concepts
- **Fiat Backing**: The 1:1 reserve model where each digital token is backed by a US Dollar (or cash equivalent) held in traditional banks or treasuries.
- **Attestations vs Audits**: The recurring debate over the transparency of Tether's reserves vs Circle's higher-frequency reporting.
- **Issuance & Redemption**: The process by which institutions exchange fiat for digital tokens and vice versa.
- **Network Distribution**: The multi-chain presence of both tokens (Ethereum, Solana, Tron, etc.) and their specific use cases in DeFi and cross-border payments.
