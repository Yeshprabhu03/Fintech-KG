# Marqeta Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of Marqeta, the modern issuer processor that enabled companies like DoorDash, Instacart, and Square to create highly custom card programs.

## Key Concepts
- **JIT (Just-in-Time) Funding**: Marqeta's core differentiator, allowing a merchant to authorize a transaction against their own server in real-time.
- **Dynamic Spend Controls**: Restricting a card's usage to specific merchants, amounts, or geographic ranges at the moment of swipe.
- **Developer-First API**: Pivoting the card processing industry away from legacy batch files toward real-time REST APIs.
- **Partner Ecosystem**: Its role as the primary infrastructure for many leading "Buy Now Pay Later" and gig economy platforms.
