# Chime Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of Chime, the leading US consumer neobank, which pioneered the "Early Direct Deposit" and "SpotMe" features.

## Key Concepts
- **Interchange Monetization**: How Chime's business model relies almost entirely on the debit interchange generated by its customers' spending.
- **Sponsor Bank Relationship**: Chime's reliance on The Bancorp Bank and Stride Bank to provide the underlying regulated infrastructure.
- **Credit Builder Card**: A secured credit card designed to help low-to-middle income (LMI) consumers build their credit history.
- **Member Acquisition**: Targeting the segment of the market that felt underserved or "fee-gouged" by traditional big banks.
