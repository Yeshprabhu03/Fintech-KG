# Razorpay Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of Razorpay, the "Stripe of India," which has expanded from a payment gateway into a full-stack financial services platform for Indian businesses.

## Key Concepts
- **UPI Integration**: How Razorpay optimized its stack to handle the massive volume and unique characteristics of India's Unified Payments Interface.
- **RazorpayX (Neo-banking)**: Moving from payment collection to business payouts, payroll, and vendor payments.
- **Compliance & Local Regulation**: Navigating the RBI's strict guidelines on payment aggregators and data residency.
- **Acquiring for SMEs**: Providing institutional-grade payment infrastructure to small and medium-sized enterprises in India.
