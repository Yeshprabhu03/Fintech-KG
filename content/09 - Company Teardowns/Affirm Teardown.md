## Summary
An analysis of Affirm, the high-end "Buy Now Pay Later" provide that focuses on large-ticket items and transparent, no-late-fee lending.

## Key Concepts
- **Transparent Lending**: Affirm's rejection of late fees and focus on simple interest transparency for the consumer.
- **Merchant Partnerships**: The significance of the Peloton and Amazon partnerships in driving Affirm's GMV and market position.
- **Funding Strategy**: How Affirm uses a mix of venture equity, warehouse lines, and securitization to fund its loan book.
- **Adaptive Checkout**: Dynamically offering different loan terms (Pay-in-4 vs. monthly installments) based on the purchase amount and risk profile.
