## Summary
Fifth Third Bank is preparing for the integration of Comerica Bank's customer base onto its platforms, with a target completion date around Labor Day. The bank is conducting dress rehearsals to ensure a smooth transition for Comerica customers.

## Fintech Implications
This type of system integration is crucial to banking-as-a-service and white-labeled banking products. If larger institutions can successfully acquire and integrate other banks while minimizing disruption to customers, it enables them to efficiently broaden their customer base and product offerings, which can then be leveraged as a backend service for fintech companies.
