# Brex Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of Brex, the fintech that redefined the corporate card for startups by underwriting based on cash-on-hand rather than personal credit scores.

## Key Concepts
- **Alternative Underwriting**: How Brex leveraged its visibility into a startup's bank account (often via Plaid) to provide 10x higher credit limits than traditional banks.
- **Vertical Landing**: Starting as the "corporate card for YC startups" before expanding into a broader financial operating system for businesses.
- **Brex Empowerment**: Moving into software-as-a-service with spend management and travel tools to increase customer stickiness and LTV.
- **The "Pivot" Away from SMB**: Lessons from Brex's controversial decision to stop serving smaller, non-equity-backed businesses in 2022.
