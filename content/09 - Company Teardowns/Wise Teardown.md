# Wise Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
A deep-dive into how Wise (formerly TransferWise) successfully built a global parallel payment system to SWIFT.

## Key Concepts
- **Direct Ledger Integration**: How Wise connects directly to central banks (e.g. Bank of England RTGS) to lower costs.
- **Multicurrency Accounts**: The technical difficulty of maintaining 40+ local bank details for one customer.
- **Revenue Model**: Transparent FX markup + fee-based transactions.
- **Regulatory Strategy**: Obtaining local money transmitter licenses in every operational jurisdiction.
