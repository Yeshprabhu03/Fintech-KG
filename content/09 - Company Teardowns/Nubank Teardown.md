# Nubank Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of Nubank, the world's largest digital bank by customer count, focusing on its rapid expansion in Latin America.

## Key Concepts
- **Low CAC, High Engagement**: How Nubank achieved massive growth in Brazil with almost zero marketing spend by focusing on user experience and customer service.
- **Credit Card Entry Strategy**: Starting with a "purple" no-fee credit card to build a brand before expanding into full banking services.
- **Unit Economics**: Achieving profitability by serving millions of underbanked customers with a low-cost operating model.
- **Expansion Strategy**: Applying the Brazil playbook to Mexico and Colombia.
