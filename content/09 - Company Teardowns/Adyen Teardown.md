# Adyen Teardown

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
An analysis of Adyen, the Dutch payment giant that achieved massive scale by building its own global network of processing and acquiring from the ground up.

## Key Concepts
- **Single Platform Architecture**: Avoiding the "Franken-stack" problem by building a unified platform for gateway, risk, and acquiring.
- **Direct Acquiring**: Adyen's strategy of obtaining banking and acquiring licenses in major markets (US, Europe, Brazil) to cut out intermediaries.
- **Enterprise Focus**: Serving high-volume global merchants (Netflix, Uber, Spotify) with a focus on authorization rate optimization.
- **Profitability & Scalability**: How Adyen's capital-efficient growth model differs from traditional VC-backed fintechs.
