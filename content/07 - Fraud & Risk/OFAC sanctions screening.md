# OFAC sanctions screening

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The process of checking individuals, entities, and countries against lists managed by the Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) to prevent transactions with prohibited parties.

## Key Concepts
- **Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) List**: The primary list of individuals and companies owned or controlled by, or acting for or on behalf of, targeted countries.
- **Fuzzy Matching**: The technical challenge of matching names that may have different spellings, transliterations, or aliases (e.g., Mohammad vs. Muhammad).
- **Secondary Sanctions**: The risk to non-US institutions if they engage in significant transactions with parties on the OFAC list.
- **Blocking vs. Rejecting**: The specific regulatory actions required when a SDN hit occurs — typically freezing the funds and reporting the hit to OFAC.
