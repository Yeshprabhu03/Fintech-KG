## Summary

## Key Concepts
- **Bank Secrecy Act (BSA)**: The legislative foundation of US AML efforts.
- **SAR Filing**: Suspicious Activity Reports and when they are required.
- **KYC Layers**: Customer Identification Program (CIP) vs Customer Due Diligence (CDD).
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Automated systems to detect money laundering patterns in real-time.
