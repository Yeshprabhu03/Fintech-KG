# Visa Direct vs Mastercard Send

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The "push payment" platforms developed by the two largest card networks to facilitate real-time funds transfers using existing card infrastructure (OCTs).

## Key Concepts
- **Original Credit Transaction (OCT)**: The technical mechanism that allows a merchant or sender to push money *to* a cardholder.
- **Push vs. Pull**: How these networks differ from the traditional "pull" model used in merchant acquiring.
- **Use Cases**: Gig economy payouts, P2P transfers (Venmo/Cash App), and insurance disbursements.
- **Network Interoperability**: How Visa and Mastercard compete to capture non-ISO (non-retail) payment volume.
