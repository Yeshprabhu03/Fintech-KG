# ACH Network mechanics

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The Automated Clearing House (ACH) is the backbone of the US credit and debit system, facilitating batch-based electronic funds transfers between bank accounts.

## Key Concepts
- **Batch Processing**: Unlike RTP, ACH transactions are grouped and processed in cycles throughout the day.
- **NACHA Rules**: The governance framework managed by the National Automated Clearing House Association.
- **Settlement Windows**: Standard ACH (2-3 days), Same-Day ACH, and the role of the Federal Reserve and EPN.
- **Returns & Reversals**: The unique mechanisms for reversing transactions due to NSF or unauthorized activity.
