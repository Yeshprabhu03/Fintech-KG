# Acquiring economics

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The study of how merchant acquirers and payment processors monetize the acceptance of digital payments, involving the spread between merchant fees and network costs.

## Key Concepts
- **MDR (Merchant Discount Rate)**: The total percentage fee a merchant pays to accept a card transaction.
- **Interchange-Plus Pricing**: A transparent model where processors pass through network costs and add a fixed markup.
- **Tiered Pricing**: A bundle of transactions (Qualified, Mid-Qual, Non-Qual) often used to obscure real costs.
- **Merchant Acquiring Stack**: The roles of the ISO (Independent Sales Organization), Payment Gateway, and Acquirer Bank.
