# SEPA Instant (Europe)

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The SEPA Instant Credit Transfer (SCT Inst) scheme facilitates pan-European instant payments in Euros, allowing funds to be available in less than 10 seconds.

## Key Concepts
- **SCT Inst Scheme**: The ruleset established by the European Payments Council (EPC).
- **Interoperability**: Challenges in connecting different CSMs (Clearing and Settlement Mechanisms) across Europe.
- **TIPS & RT1**: The primary settlement platforms (Eurosystem's TIPS and EBA Clearing's RT1).
- **Mandatory Adoption**: The EU's movement toward making instant payment capabilities mandatory for all PSPs.
