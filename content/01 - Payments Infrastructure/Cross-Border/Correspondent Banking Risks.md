# Correspondent Banking Risks

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The vulnerabilities and systematic risks inherent in the legacy "bank-to-bank" model of global value transfer.

## Key Concepts
- **De-risking**: Why global banks are cutting ties with local banks in higher-risk regions.
- **Correspondent Banking Decline**: The shrinking network of banks willing to facilitate cross-border SWIFT transfers.
- **Remittance Impact**: How de-risking makes it harder and more expensive for individuals to send money home.
- **Emerging Alternatives**: Who is filling the gap? (Fintechs, stablecoin rails, and private networks).
