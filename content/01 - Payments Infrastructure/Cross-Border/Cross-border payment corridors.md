# Cross-border payment corridors

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The specific geographic routes through which money flows across borders (e.g., US-to-Mexico, UAE-to-India). This note analyzes the economics and friction of these high-volume paths.

## Key Concepts
- **Route Liquidity**: Why some corridors are cheap (competitive) while others are prohibitively expensive.
- **Remittance Hubs**: The role of global centers like the US, UAE, and Singapore in originating cross-border flow.
- **FX Spread & Fees**: The two primary ways intermediaries monetize cross-border value transfer.
- **Corridor-Specific Regulation**: How anti-money laundering (AML) requirements vary significantly across different corridors.
