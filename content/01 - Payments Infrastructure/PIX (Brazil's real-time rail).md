# PIX (Brazil's real-time rail)

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The instant payment platform created and managed by the Central Bank of Brazil, which has become the dominant method of payment in the country.

## Key Concepts
- **QR Code Payments**: PIX's reliance on standardized QR codes for both P2P and P2M transactions.
- **Participation Requirement**: Why the Central Bank mandated participation for all large banks.
- **Financial Inclusion**: How PIX brought millions of unbanked Brazilians into the digital financial ecosystem.
- **Real-time Settlement**: Settlement happens in seconds on the Central Bank's own ledger.
