# Embedded investments

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The integration of stock trading, crypto investments, or automated wealth management into non-financial apps (e.g., a budgeting app or an employer benefits platform).

## Key Concepts
- **Fractional Shares**: The underlying technology that allows users to invest small amounts ($1 or less) into expensive stocks.
- **Round-up Investing**: Automatically investing the "spare change" from card transactions into a user's portfolio.
- **Wealth-as-a-Service**: The API-driven platforms (e.g., DriveWealth, Apex Clearing) that provide the brokerage infrastructure for embedded investing.
- **RIA (Registered Investment Advisor) Compliance**: The complex regulatory requirements for platforms offering personalized financial advice.
