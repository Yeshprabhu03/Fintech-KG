# Middleware players (Unit, Treasury Prime, Synctera)

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The "connective tissue" of the BaaS ecosystem—platforms that provide the APIs, ledgering, and compliance tools that allow fintechs to connect to legacy banks.

## Key Concepts
- **API Orchestration**: Solving the "legacy tech" problem by wrapping old core banking systems in modern REST APIs.
- **Sub-Ledgering**: Maintaining the "source of truth" for consumer balances while the actual funds sit in a program bank's pooled account.
- **Compliance-as-a-Service**: Providing the software (KYC, AML monitoring) that helps the bank supervise the fintech's activity.
- **Direct vs. Indirect Models**: Comparing platforms like Unit (where they manage the bank relationship) vs. Synctera/Treasury Prime (where the fintech often contracts with the bank directly).
