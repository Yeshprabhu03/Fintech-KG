# Issuer processor landscape

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The specialized technology companies that connect a fintech's ledger to the card networks (Visa/Mastercard) and manage the lifecycle of a physical or virtual card.

## Key Concepts
- **Core Processing**: Managing card authorizations, balances, and transaction history in real-time.
- **Just-in-Time (JIT) Funding**: The ability for a processor (e.g., Marqeta) to check a fintech's external ledger at the moment of a transaction.
- **Legacy vs. Modern**: Comparing established players like FIS/Fiserv to modern cloud-native processors like Marqeta, Lithic, and Highnote.
- **Value-Added Services**: Program management, fraud mitigation, and dispute handling provided by the processor.
