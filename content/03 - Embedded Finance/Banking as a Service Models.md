# Banking as a Service Models

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The architectural tiers and business models that allow non-bank platforms to offer financial services.

## Key Concepts
- **BaaS Stack Tiers**: License (Bank) vs. Ledger (Tech) vs. User Experience (Fintech).
- **Revenue Models**: Account fees, interchange share, and interest-rate arbitrage.
- **Regulatory Risk**: The shift from light-touch oversight to strict direct-bank supervision.
- **Market Players**: Unit, Treasury Prime, Bond, and the "Direct-to-Bank" scaling model.
