# Embedded lending

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The integration of credit and lending products directly into non-financial platforms, allowing users to access financing at the point of need (e.g., checkout or business dashboard).

## Key Concepts
- **Contextual Underwriting**: Using platform data (e.g., Shopify sales history) to assess creditworthiness instead of relying solely on bureau scores.
- **Point of Sale (POS) Financing**: Providing loans at the moment of purchase, often categorized as BNPL (Buy Now Pay Later).
- **Embedded B2B Lending**: Working capital loans or lines of credit integrated into SaaS platforms for small businesses.
- **Revenue Share**: How platforms and financial partners split the interest and fees generated by embedded loans.
