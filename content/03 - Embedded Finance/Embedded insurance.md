# Embedded insurance

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The integration of insurance products into a customer's purchase journey on a non-insurance platform, such as travel insurance during a flight booking.

## Key Concepts
- **Usage-Based Insurance (UBI)**: Policies that are activated only when needed or based on specific behavior.
- **Parametric Insurance**: Coverage that pays out automatically when a specific, measurable event occurs (e.g., a delayed flight) without a complex claims process.
- **Affinity Groups**: Leveraging a platform's existing user base to offer tailored insurance products (e.g., e-commerce insurance for Shopify merchants).
- **MGA (Managing General Agent)**: The specialized entities that Bridge the gap between tech platforms and traditional insurance carriers.
