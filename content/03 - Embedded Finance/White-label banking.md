# White-label banking

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The provision of branded bank accounts, debit cards, and core financial services by a technology platform that is NOT the underlying chartered bank.

## Key Concepts
- **Branding vs. Charter**: The distinction between the consumer-facing app (fintech) and the back-end "program bank."
- **Customer Relationship**: Who owns the customer? (Typically the fintech owns the relationship, but the bank owns the account).
- **Onboarding & KYC**: Why white-label platforms must still clear banking-standard identity verification.
- **Vertical Banking**: Industry-specific banking (e.g., banking for creators, banking for contractors) powered by white-label infrastructure.
