# Jobs to be done in fintech

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The application of Clayton Christensen's "Jobs to be Done" framework to financial services — moving beyond demographics to understand why customers "hire" a specific fintech product.

## Key Concepts
- **Core Jobs**: Common financial jobs such as "Moving value," "Storing value," "Growing value," and "Protecting value."
- **Functional vs. Emotional Jobs**: The difference between the utility of an app (e.g., getting a loan) and the emotional feeling it provides (e.g., peace of mind, status).
- **Competitors as Substitutes**: Recognizing that a neobank doesn't just compete with other banks, but with cash, Excel, and physical envelopes.
- **The "Big Hire" vs "Little Hire"**: The initial decision to sign up for an app vs. the daily decision to use its specific features.
