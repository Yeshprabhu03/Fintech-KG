# Basel III Capital Requirements

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The global regulatory framework for bank capital adequacy, stress testing, and market liquidity risk.

## Key Concepts
- **Tier 1 Capital**: The core capital a bank holds, primarily in reserves and highly liquid assets.
- **Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR)**: Ensuring banks have enough cash to survive a 30-day stress scenario.
- **Fintech Impact**: How stricter requirements for big banks have pushed risky lending toward fintechs and shadow banks.
- **Reserves**: How much cash a bank must keep relative to its loans.
