# CRA (Community Reinvestment Act)

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The landmark US law designed to encourage commercial banks and savings associations to help meet the needs of borrowers in all segments of their communities, including low- and moderate-income neighborhoods.

## Key Concepts
- **Assessment Areas**: The geographic regions (usually around physical branches) where a bank is evaluated on its lending.
- **Fintech & Digital Banking Challenge**: How the rise of branchless banking and BaaS models has complicated the "geographic" definition of CRA compliance.
- **CRA Modernization**: Recent regulatory updates to account for digital delivery channels and cross-jurisdictional lending.
- **Ratings**: Needs to Improve, Satisfactory, and Outstanding, and their impact on a bank's ability to merge or acquire other banks.
