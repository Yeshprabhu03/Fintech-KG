# FDIC insurance mechanics

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The system provided by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation to protect depositors if an FDIC-insured bank fails.

## Key Concepts
- **Coverage Limits**: The standard insurance amount ($250,000 per depositor, per insured bank, for each account ownership category).
- **Sweep Accounts**: How fintechs use "program banks" to provide multi-million dollar FDIC insurance by spreading funds across many institutions.
- **DIF (Deposit Insurance Fund)**: The fund maintained by assessments on insured banks that pays out when a bank fails.
- **Fintech Pass-through**: The legal and operational requirements for fintechs to ensure their customers' funds are truly "pass-through" insured.
