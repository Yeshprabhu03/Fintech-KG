# Consent order process

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The formal legal document issued by a regulator (like the OCC, FDIC, or Fed) when they identify significant deficiencies in a bank's operations, particularly in compliance or risk management.

## Key Concepts
- **Enforcement Action**: The transition from minor "Matters Requiring Attention" (MRAs) to a public, binding consent order.
- **Remediation Plan**: The detailed roadmap a bank must submit to prove they are fixing the identified issues.
- **Growth Caps**: Common penalties in consent orders that prevent a bank from taking on new BaaS partners or opening new branches.
- **Impact on Fintechs**: Why a consent order at a sponsor bank can be a "death sentence" or significant growth hurdle for its fintech partners.
