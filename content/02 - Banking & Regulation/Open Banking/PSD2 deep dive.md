# PSD2 deep dive

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The Second Payment Services Directive (PSD2) is the regulatory framework in the European Union that catalyzed the transition toward open banking.

## Key Concepts
- **AISPs & PISPs**: Account Information Service Providers (data access) and Payment Initiation Service Providers (initiating transfers).
- **Strong Customer Authentication (SCA)**: Mandatory multi-factor authentication for most European digital payments.
- **Open APIs**: The requirement for banks to provide secure interfaces so third-party providers can access customer account data (with consent).
- **PSD3 Outlook**: How the upcoming PSD3 and PSR (Payment Services Regulation) aim to fix the fragmentation in PSD2 implementation.
