# Section 1071 small business lending

> ⚠️ Draft — needs verification against primary sources

## Summary
The CFPB's rule implementing Section 1071 of the Dodd-Frank Act, which requires financial institutions to collect and report data on lending to small businesses, particularly those that are minority-owned or women-owned.

## Key Concepts
- **Data Collection Requirements**: Capturing information on business type, credit applied for, and demographic data of business owners.
- **Fair Lending Oversight**: Providing the CFPB with data to identify and address potential discrimination in small business lending.
- **Compliance Burden**: The significant operational challenge for banks and fintech lenders to update their origination workflows to capture these data points.
- **Exemptions**: Which institutions are covered by the rule based on their small business loan volume.
